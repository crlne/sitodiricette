Veggie

Mozzarella & avocado salad


When life gives you avocados, you make an avocado salad.


Ingredients


2 x 6 cherry tomatoes on the vine
1 clove garlic, chopped
4 tsp extra virgin olive oil
200g Waitrose Burrata cheese (vegetarian) or Garofalo Buffalo’s Mozzarella (not vegetarian), drained and sliced
1 Perfectly Ripe Avocado, peeled, stoned and sliced
10 large fresh basil leaves, torn
2 tsp balsamic vinegar


Directions


Step 1

Preheat the oven to 220°C, gas mark 7. Place the tomatoes in a small roasting tin.
Scatter with the garlic, olive oil and seasoning.
Bake for 15 minutes until the tomato skins are bursting.

Step 2

Meanwhile, share the cheese slices between 2 serving plates.
Spoon the hot tomatoes and their juice over the slices.
Place the avocado pieces next to the cheese, then scatter with the torn basil leaves and a drizzle of the balsamic vinegar.
Serve with ciabatta to mop up the juices.
